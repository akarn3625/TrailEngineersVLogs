About Engineers Vlogs

Engineers Vlogs is a group of qualified engineers formed with the mindset and slogan "Let's Bring Engineering out of The Text Books". We focus on hands-on learning—workshops, trainings, project resources and community support for engineering aspirants.

Engineers Vlogs

Engineers Vlogs primarily focuses on technical-learning aspects and skills development by practical approach rather than theoretical and bookish learning methods. We aim to be an integral part of engineering aspirants and students by facilitating them with news and information, workshops, trainings, study materials and project resources.

We strive to be committed to the community we serve—our dedicated team, talented faculty and staff. We take pride in our active partners, visionary parents, well-wishers, and friends.

Message from the Chairman

As a Chairman, I have articulated our mission with clarity: Engineers Vlogs is dedicated to providing students with comprehensive training in STEAM, robotics, and other relevant fields to enrich their practical understanding, thereby narrowing the gap between academia and the practicality of the corporate world. Our aim is global reach and impact.

In our relentless pursuit of excellence, we continuously refine our methodologies, embracing the latest technologies and organizational strategies to better serve diverse learners—from individuals to institutions.

Ashutosh Karn — Founder / Chairman
